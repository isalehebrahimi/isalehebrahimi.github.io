About ME

I am Saleh, a 4th-year Computer Science student.
I've always been interested in Artificial Intelligence, so, after three years of studying Mechanical Engineering, I changed my major to Computer Science to pursue my goals in every day evolving field of AI. (I'm a massive fan of training machines to make them smarter and more reliable.)
Because of my vast interest in the broad field of Machine Learning, I started researching in this field as a research assistant back in 2018, my 3rd and 4th semester. I've continued to do my research in 2019, which brought me the Distinguished researcher award in 2020.
I've started my research in the Data Mining field, and now, my research interests mainly consist of Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing, and Data mining.
